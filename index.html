Welcome to the Online Degree Audit.

This online degree audit is a helpful tool for COEN majors that will clearly and effectively indicate whether or not you are on track to graduate.

Click here to go to the degree audit.

How To Use

Clicking on the link provided above will lead you to the interactive degree audit. Proceed to follow these steps:
To create a new degree audit:
1. Choose "New Audit" when prompted.
2. Enter COEN major classes and CORE classes.
3. Press "Update" to see updated progress.

To load existing degree audit:
1. Choose "Load Existing" when prompted.
2. Paste link into text box.
